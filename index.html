M-Tech Project

Study of Spice based oils for their use in Cancer studies. Anticancer properties of 12 oils against 5 OncoProteins were studied for their binding energies using Docking studies.Interaction studies were performed to analyse the nature and type of interactions within the protein ligand bond.Finally good results were obtained with Coriander and Cinnamon Leaf for comparison with synthetic drugs for replacement using Pharmacophore Modelling.Nanoemulsions of these good performance oils were prepared for Cytotoxicity Tests.
